# Corporate

**Version:** 3.0
**Status:** Approved
**Module:** Core

---

# Definition

A **Corporate** is the highest organizational Entity within RF-ONE.

It represents the legal organization responsible for the governance, ownership and long-term strategy of the business ecosystem.

A Corporate defines **why** the organization exists.
Operational Units define **how** business operations are executed.

---

# Purpose

The Corporate exists to:

- Define corporate strategy
- Own organizational assets
- Govern Brands
- Govern Operational Units
- Establish corporate policies
- Allocate corporate resources
- Manage investments
- Ensure legal and regulatory compliance
- Protect intellectual property

Operational execution is delegated to Operational Units.

---

# Position in the Core

```
Corporate
    ↓
Brand
    ↓
Operational Unit
    ↓
Operational Area
```

---

# Identity

Every Corporate has a unique immutable RF-ONE identifier.

Mutable attributes may include:

- Legal Name
- Headquarters
- Ownership Structure
- Executive Team
- Tax Status
- Contact Information

---

# Governance

Corporate defines:

- Vision
- Mission
- Strategic Objectives
- Governance Rules
- Corporate Standards
- Enterprise Policies

These rules apply across every Brand and Operational Unit.

---

# Ownership

Corporate may own Brands, Operational Units, Intellectual Property, Investments, Corporate Funds, Software Licenses, Trademarks, Patents and Corporate Documentation.

Ownership and operational responsibility remain independent concepts.

---

# Relationships

## Brand

A Corporate owns one or more Brands.

Each Brand belongs to exactly one Corporate.

## Operational Unit

Operational Units belong to one Corporate and execute business activities on its behalf.

---

# Corporate Functions

Typical centralized functions include Executive Management, Finance, Human Resources, Legal, Information Technology, Business Development, Corporate Marketing and Compliance.

---

# Corporate Policies

Corporate defines enterprise-wide policies.

Domain-specific policies belong to their respective Domain.

---

# Business Rules

- Every RF-ONE installation contains exactly one Corporate.
- Every Brand belongs to one Corporate.
- Every Operational Unit belongs to one Corporate.
- Corporate defines governance and strategy.
- Operational Units execute business operations.
- Corporate identity is immutable.
- Ownership and operational responsibility are distinct concepts.

---

# Scalability

The model supports single-location organizations, multi-location organizations, multi-brand organizations, franchise networks, holding companies and international organizations without changes to the Core.
