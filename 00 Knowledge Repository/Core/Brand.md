# Brand

**Version:** 3.0
**Status:** Approved
**Module:** Core

---

# Definition

A **Brand** is the commercial identity through which a Corporate presents itself to the market.

It defines how customers recognize, experience and remember the organization, independently of its legal structure or operational implementation.

A Brand represents the promise made to the market.

---

# Purpose

A Brand exists to define:

- Market Positioning
- Brand Identity
- Customer Experience
- Communication Style
- Brand Values
- Product Philosophy
- Service Standards

Operational Units deliver these standards.

---

```
Corporate
```

---

# Identity

Every Brand has a unique immutable RF-ONE identifier.

Mutable attributes may include:

- Brand Name
- Logo
- Visual Identity
- Communication Strategy
- Target Market
- Brand Guidelines

---

# Relationships

## Corporate

Every Brand belongs to exactly one Corporate.

Corporate owns the Brand and defines its strategic direction.

## Operational Unit

A Brand is implemented through one or more Operational Units.

Every Operational Unit belongs to exactly one Brand.

---

# Brand Standards

A Brand defines enterprise-wide standards including:

- Customer Experience
- Communication
- Visual Identity
- Product Standards
- Service Standards
- Quality Standards

Domain-specific standards (recipes, menus, food presentation, etc.) belong to the corresponding Domain.

---

# Intellectual Property

A Brand represents the commercial use of Corporate-owned intellectual property including:

- Trademarks
- Logos
- Slogans
- Brand Guidelines
- Marketing Assets

---

# Business Rules

- Every Brand belongs to exactly one Corporate.
- Every Operational Unit belongs to exactly one Brand.
- A Brand may be implemented by multiple Operational Units.
- A Brand defines identity, not operations.
- Operational Units execute Brand standards.

---

# Scalability

The Brand model supports:

- Single-brand organizations
- Multi-brand organizations
- Franchise networks
- Virtual brands
- Future commercial concepts

without requiring changes to the Core.
