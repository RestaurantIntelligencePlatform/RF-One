# Purpose

This document defines the objectives that drive every decision made by RF-ONE.

Objectives answer one question:

> "Why does RF-ONE exist?"

They are independent of any specific module or technology.

---

# OBJ-0001 — Primary Objective

## Title

Maximize Economic Profit

## Description

RF-ONE exists to maximize the economic profit of the business.

This is the primary objective of the entire platform and takes precedence over all functional decisions.

---

# OBJ-0002 — Profit Definition

## Title

Cash-Based Profit

## Description

For RF-ONE, profit is defined as:

> Cash received minus cash paid.

The primary economic metric of the platform is therefore the net cash generated by the business during the selected period.

Accounting indicators may be used as supporting information but are not considered the primary optimization target.

---

# OBJ-0003 — Optimization Scope

## Title

Unlimited Optimization Scope

## Description

RF-ONE may optimize any aspect of the business that can increase economic profit.

This includes, but is not limited to:

- Revenue
- Food Cost
- Labor Cost
- Purchasing
- Inventory
- Pricing
- Marketing
- Productivity
- Waste Reduction
- Energy Consumption
- Maintenance
- Automation
- Business Processes

The optimization scope is intentionally unrestricted.

---

# OBJ-0004 — Legal Constraints

## Title

Legal Compliance

## Description

RF-ONE may recommend any action that increases economic profit provided that the action complies with applicable laws and with any business-specific constraints configured by the customer.

The platform itself does not impose a business philosophy.

---

# OBJ-0005 — Operational and Strategic Decisions

## Title

Decision Time Horizon

## Description

RF-ONE supports two categories of decisions.

### Operational Decisions

Operational recommendations should generate measurable economic benefits as early as possible after implementation.

### Strategic Decisions

Strategic initiatives may have different investment horizons.

The acceptable return period depends on the specific strategy and must be evaluated accordingly.

---

# OBJ-0006 — Success Measurement

## Title

Measure Generated Value

## Description

The success of RF-ONE is measured by the economic benefit generated through its recommendations.

Whenever possible, the platform should compare:

- the actual results obtained after a recommendation was adopted;

against

- the estimated outcome had the recommendation not been implemented.

The objective is to continuously quantify the value created by RF-ONE.
